Fully-Automatic Capping Machine

The Fully-Automatic Capping Machine is an essential component in the liquid filling industry. After cartridges are filled, the cap- ping process is crucial for secure storage and transportation. Our machine ensures consistent, precise pressure during the cap- ping process, eliminating the risk of uneven force applied manually, which can result in poor airtight seals. With its advanced technology, this machine provides reliable, uniform sealing every time, ensuring optimal performance and reducing the potential for leakage or contamination during handling and shipping.
