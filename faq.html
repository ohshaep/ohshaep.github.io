Q: What does Longwill specialize in?
A: Longwill focuses on high-precision filling and capping machines for the electronic cigarette industry. The company is dedicated to reducing labor costs,
increasing production efficiency, and providing customizable solutions tailored to client needs.
Q: How can I contact Longwill for inquiries?
A: You can visit their official website at https://longwillmachinery.com/about/ or contact their customer service team for support.
Q: What types of filling machines does Longwill offer?
A: Longwill provides two main types of filling machines:
Semi-Automatic Cartridge and Device Filling Machine: Ideal for smaller-scale operations and manual handling.
Fully-Automatic Cartridge and Device Filling Machine: Designed for high-volume production with minimal manual intervention.
Q: What materials are used in Longwill filling machines?
A: The machines use 316 medical-grade stainless steel for components like liquid output needles, ensuring hygiene, durability, and reduced oil loss.
Q: Are the machines easy to operate and clean?
A: Yes, Longwill machines are designed with user-friendliness in mind. They are easy to operate, clean, and maintain, making them ideal for consistent use.
Q: Can the filling machines be customized?
A: Absolutely. Longwill offers fully customizable filling machines to meet specific client needs, including unique tray designs for holding cartridges or containers during operation.
Q: What accessories are available for filling machines?
A: Longwill provides a variety of accessories, including medical-grade tubing for liquid transfer, high-quality stainless steel needles, and custom trays.
Q: Do the filling machines come with an operation manual?
A: Yes, all Longwill machines come with a detailed operation manual, as well as training resources to help users maximize efficiency.
