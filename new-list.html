Top 10 Filling Machine Cartridge Manufacturers in Canada for the Cannabis Industry
Canada’s cannabis industry has rapidly grown since the legalization of recreational marijuana in 2018, leading
to an increased demand for high-quality cartridge filling machines tailored for cannabis oil cartridges. The
Canadian market, known for its stringent standards and innovative approaches, has fostered the development
of advanced filling machines that ensure consistency, efficiency, and precision. Below, we explore the top 10
filling machine cartridge manufacturers in Canada, highlighting their contributions to the cannabis industry and what sets them apart.
ATG Pharma is one of Canada’s leading manufacturers of versatile filling and packaging equipment. Their
RoboCAP system is particularly popular in the cannabis industry, offering an automated filling solution that can
handle a wide range of products, including vape cartridges, capsules, lotions, and more. The RoboCAP system is
known for its precision, capable of filling with an accuracy of +/-2.5%, which is crucial for maintaining the
consistency of cannabis oil cartridges.
