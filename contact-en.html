About Astribot

Astribot was founded in Shenzhen in December 2022. The company has pioneered a software - hardware integrated system architecture for AI (Design for AI, DFAI), deeply coupling ‘AI intelligence’ with 'optimal manipulation'.

This innovation has led to key breakthroughs in embodied intelligence and data acquisition, enabling robots to learn, think, and work like humans while fostering smooth and intelligent interactions with people.
