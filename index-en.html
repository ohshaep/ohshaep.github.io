About Us

Astribot was founded in Shenzhen in December 2022. As a leading AI robotics company, our vision is to empower billions of people with Al robotic partners, and our mission is to promote co-existence, co-creation and co-prosperity between humans and robots.

The company has pioneered a software-hardware integrated system architecture for AI (Design for AI, DFAI), deeply coupling 'AI intelligence' with 'optimal manipulation' . This innovation has led to key breakthroughs in embodied intelligence and data acquisition, enabling robots to learn, think, and work like humans while fostering smooth and intelligent interactions with people.
