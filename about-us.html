About Us

Longwill Technology, founded in 2014, specializes in manufacturing high-quality filling and capping machines for e-cigarette oil and CBD oil industries. We provide both fully automat- ic and semi-automatic solutions, including reliable cartridge filling and capping machines. Led by Chief Engineer James, with 20+ years of industry expertise, we are committed to in- novation and efficiency, serving clients worldwide.

Longwill Certificates

All machines have passed CE and FCC certifications, allowing them to be sold in most countries worldwide. Additionally, the silicone tubing has passed the LFGB
test in Germany, the pumps and oil barrels have passed the FDA test in the United States, and all materials in contact with the oil are safe.
